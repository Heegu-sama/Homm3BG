\addsection{Game Modes}{\skills/intelligence.png}

\begin{multicols*}{2}

Each game of Heroes III is played using a Scenario from the Mission Book.\par 
The core game only allows you to choose scenarios for 1-3 players. For 4 or more players, expansions are required.\par
In addition to the scenario objectives, read the \pagelink{Scenario end}{general victory conditions} depending on the scenario type.

\subsection*{Clash}
A fully competitive mode. The opponent to your right \pagelink{combat}{controls} neutral enemy units.

\subsection*{Campaign}
A single player mode\index{Solo Mode} of interconnected Scenarios against an enemy AI.
To control the enemy units, follow \pagelink{AIrules}{the AI rules}.
Further rules changes are detailed in the Campaign Mission Books.

\subsection*{Alliance}
Usually a 2 vs.~2 team-based mode. The enemy team \pagelink{combat}{controls} neutral enemy units and allied players have expanded opportunities to \pagelink{Trading}{trade}. 

\subsection*{Cooperative}
A Cooperative mode where everyone shares the same goal. \pagelink{AIRules}{The AI rules} in combat and the extended \pagelink{Trading}{trading rules} applies here as well.

\subsection*{Tournament}
A 1 vs.~1 mode with different way of building map and scoring. Described in the Tournament Book.

\subsection*{Skirmish and Adventure}
Two more 1 vs.~1 modes added by the Battlefield expansion. Described in Battlefield expansion's rulebook.
